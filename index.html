Executive Dysfunction Cleaning Buddy

This program is designed to assist those with executive function (EF) deficiencies manage their homes and clean their room in easy to follow, efficient steps. The user is guided through a low-stress, chunked method of cleaning where they focus on one specific area at a time for 5 minutes each- this helps the user stay calm and avoid becoming overwhelmed. Timed breaks are also incorporated in order to allow the user a chance to decompress and regroup.

Just choose where you want to clean and the desired amount of time to break, then click "Let's Go!" in order to get started!

Here are some tips for cleaning:

  • Don't get tunnel vision (Getting stuck on one detail while ignoring the rest)
  • Use two separate bags for recycle and trash
  • Do not attempt to clean anywhere other than the current area
  • STAY OFF YOUR PHONE!!!!!
  • Open the window blinds and let in some sun
  • Listen to music
  • Have fun! 😄
